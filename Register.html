  2. Formal admission to the University of Oklahoma

Process to Register

  1. Self-identify as a student with a disability and submit documentation to the Disability Resource Center. In Higher Education, it is your responsibility as a student to self-identify as an individual with a disability. Information regarding documentation can be found at Documentation.

  2. Once your documentation has been received, it will be reviewed within 5-15 business days. Please check your OU email account regularly because this is how we will contact you once your documentation has been reviewed.

  3. If there is a need for additional documentation, this information will be provided in the email you receive from the DRC. If, during the initial appointment an accommodation requested does not clearly descibe the relationship between the barrier and how an accommodation would provide access, additional information may be requested at the students' expense.

  4. When you receive an email from the DRC, please follow instructions on the email to set up an intake appointment.

  5. During the initial appointment, DRC staff and the student will engage in an interactive process to discuss your previous educational experience, past use of accommodations, and what has been effective in providing access. Students will also be introuced to their rights and responsbilities in postsecondary education.

  6. Accommodations ar ethen developed to promose an equal educational opportunity.
